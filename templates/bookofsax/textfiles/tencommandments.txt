The Ten Commandments 
-----------------------------------------

I. Thou shalt not date thy sax brother or sister.

II. Thou may "engage" with thy sax brother or sister, but thou shalt be friends in the morning.

III. Thy "Chosen One" shall be determined by thy sax Babes.

IV. Thy sacrificial wine is Schmidts, but only thy rookies shalt drink this wine.

V. Thou shalt look out for thy sax brothers and sisters.

VI. Thou shalt participate in thy sax warm-ups.

VII. Thou shalt remove thy neckstrap before combat.

VIII. Thou shalt watch the Blues Brothers.

IX. Thou shalt know thy sax song.

X. Thou shalt not like thy trumpets.
