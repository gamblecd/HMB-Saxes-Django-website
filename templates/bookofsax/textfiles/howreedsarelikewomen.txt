========================
How Reeds are Like Women
========================

How reeds and women are similar:

You gotta get 'em wet before they will sing.

If they can't hit the high notes, they are not worth tonguing.

The right one is out there, you just need to keep going back to the box.

They all get old after a while.

Everyone has a preference on how they are shaved.

They come in all different sizes.

Some people prefer the synthetic ones over the real ones.

You always have to break the new ones in.

The really old ones get all wrinkled.

They cost too much money.
